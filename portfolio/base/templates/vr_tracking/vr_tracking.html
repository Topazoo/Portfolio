The Concept

  • Movement is one of the least realistic parts of the current VR experience. It substitutes a button push for the physical act of walking. A device that translates physical movement to movement in virtual reality would fundamentally change the experience.


  • I set about tracking footfalls with optical mice in an ongoing attempt to map them in virtual reality.


  • This code reads the speed and direction of multiple optical mice concurrently.
