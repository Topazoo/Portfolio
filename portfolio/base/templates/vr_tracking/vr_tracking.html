Mapping Real Steps to Virtual Reality Using Optical Mice

Movement is one of the least realistic parts of the current VR experience. It substitutes a button push for the physical act of walking. A device that translates physical movement to movement in virtual reality fundamentally changes the experience.



I set about tracking footfalls with optical mice in an ongoing attempt to map them in virtual reality.



This application concurrently reads the speed and direction of multiple optical mice.
